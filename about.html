Building Systems that help our customers stay ahead

Established in 1989, Concepts & Devices has developed core competence in powerful & flexible communications infrastructure solutions for diverse businesses across all verticals. From high speed copper and fiber optic cabling solutions to converged communication & business telephony, we offer complete services for your communication needs.

Drawing from a wealth of experience built over Three Generations of Business Acumen we have earned a Pride of Place in the Industry as Dependable, Reliable and Efficient.

Our technology partners in cabling and voice are CommScope and Alcatel-Lucent. With their association combined with our over two decades of industry expertise has made us the number one reseller for CommScope in Karnataka and Tamil Nadu and hurtling towards a similar position for voice solutions with Alcatel-Lucent.
